---
title: What makes a good company policy
layout: post
---

I believe the best policies, whether information security or flexible work arrangements:

* Are a genuinely useful reference
* Are written in plain language (e.g. speak truth without jargon, clichés, or obscurity)
* Reflect actual practice and give clarifying advice
* Have just enough bones
* Can be used to support having conversations with customers and colleagues
* Are updated when leanings are made, or circumstances change
* Have a change log

Over time the best policies become the "thing" that everyone "knows".
